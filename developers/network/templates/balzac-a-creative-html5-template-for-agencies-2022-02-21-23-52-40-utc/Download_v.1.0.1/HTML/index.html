    For the truly creative agency

Balzac is a template for the creative design or ad agency looking for an eye popping, hip and modern look.

Balzac comes with a wide variety of carfully crafted pages that are ready-to-use and built to save you time. Balzac includes portfolio layouts, about layouts, blog layouts, utility layouts, e-commerce layouts and a ton of components that can be dropped into any page. If you're looking for a unique creative template, then Balzac is it!

Balzac is a complete design toolkit that gives you the tools you need to create a unique and professional looking website for you or your client.

Multiple Layouts

Balzac comes with a wide range of page layouts such as homepage layouts, portfolios, project pages, e-commerce pages, utility pages and so much more. Choose the ones you need, edit and upload.

20+ Components

Make any design stand out by using a combination of the 20+ components that come with Balzac. Accordions, tabs, buttons, progress bars, maps, pricing tables, counters and a whole lot more.

Premium Plugins

As with all our templates, Balzac comes with 8 premium in-house developed plugins such as sliders, parallax sections, fullscreen sections, and counters. When there is an update you'll be sure to get it.

Purpose-built blocks

Balzac, like all our templates, is built using a block based, responsive framework that makes designing easier. With 40+ blocks at your disposal, not only will you save time, but you can create new layouts with ease.

Awesome Forms

Build simple or complex forms on the fly. Add new fields, select boxes, checkboxes and/or radio buttons to any contact form without editing JS or PHP. Our subscribe forms seamlessly integrate with MailChimp.

Isotope

Balzac includes the popular isotope grid and sorting library which allows you to create amazing masonry grids on the fly. Choose between large, small or no margin grids, animation duration, sorting duration and more.
